What is Vibecoding?

Vibecoding is a new way of building software where you describe what you want in plain English, and AI builds it for you. Instead of learning programming syntax, you learn to communicate your ideas clearly.

The term was coined by Andrej Karpathy (co-founder of OpenAI) in 2025: "Fully give in to the vibes, embrace exponentials, and forget that the code even exists."

For ecologists, this means you can turn your data into interactive dashboards, maps, and analysis tools without spending years learning to code. Your domain expertise is the most valuable skill.

How It Works

1

Describe

Tell the AI what you want in plain English. "Build me a dashboard that shows species distributions on a map."

2

Iterate

Review what the AI built. Ask for changes: "Make the points larger and color them by conservation status."

3

Deploy

Push your app to the web with one click. Share the link with your lab, your supervisor, or the world.
